About Me:

My journey begins as a young and aspiring scientist, hoping to make a difference in the world of education. After five years teaching the young minds of tomorrow, I was enlightened by the prospects of coding.

With a passion for technology and learning, I completed a full time immersive program at The Iron Yard in Durham, switching careers from full time teacher to full time software engineer.

I aspire to do God's work as He works through me each and every day. I thrive in meeting challenges and exceeding the highest of expectations. I enjoy learning from experience and sharing my knowledge and passion with others. I want to make the world a better place by being a force of positive change
